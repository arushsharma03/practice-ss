Welcome to the Smart Campus Portal

Your one-stop portal for registering complaints, accessing resources, and staying informed about campus life. We are here to help you with every issue — big or small.

About the Helpdesk

"The MIET Smart Campus Helpdesk is a digital platform that allows students to report issues related to hostels, labs, classrooms, library, transport, and more. Our team ensures timely resolution and keeps you updated on the status of your requests."

"Our mission is to provide a seamless experience for every student, ensuring their concerns are heard and resolved promptly.

How It Works

  1. Register your complaint through the portal.
  2. Receive a ticket number for tracking.
  3. Our team reviews and addresses the issue.
  4. Get notified when the issue is resolved.
